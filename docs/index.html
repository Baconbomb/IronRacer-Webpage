About IronRacer

IronRacer is a top-down racing game inspired by the gritty car culture of the 1980s. You start out with a beat-up car in your uncle’s garage and work your way up through grassroots racing series, wrenching, sliding, and fighting for your place on the podium. The game combines realistic driving physics, manual shifting, tire wear, pit stop strategy, and garage upgrades, all wrapped in a story-driven progression where every race and every bolt matters.
